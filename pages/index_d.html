Meet TALOS.

TALOS #25309 competes in KRC, the Korea Robotics Championship hosted by FEST. The competition has the same mission and rules as the FIRST Tech Challenge, and KRC winners advance to the FIRST World Championships.

We won the 1st Inspire Award in the 2023-24 CENTERSTAGE, and we plan to improve even more in coming seasons.
